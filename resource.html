TextGrids.jl (Julia package)

I am developing a package in Julia to provide the capacities to manipulate, edit, and extract information from Praat TextGrids, in support of my current work in video data analysis.

At this (early) stage, the package supports reading and writing of TextGrids (with various encodings supported), as well as basic querying and manipulation of tiers, points and boundaries.
