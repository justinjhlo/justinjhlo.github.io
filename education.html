PhD in Linguistics, University of York

Working TitleIssues of bilingualism in forensic voice comparison

SupervisorsProf Paul Foulkes & Dr Vincent Hughes

I am currently looking at how bilingualism interacts with aspects of forensic voice comparison. Specifically, I study how the performance of variables and the differentiability of voices are influenced by changes in the language spoken and the composition of the relevant speech community.

2016
MSc in Forensic Speech Science (Distinction), University of York

DissertationDouble trouble?: The effect of bilingualism on filled pauses and their discriminatory power in forensic voice comparison

SupervisorsProf Paul Foulkes & Dr Vincent Hughes

I explored the use of filled pauses in forensic voice comparison in the bilingual context. I compared the acoustics of filled pauses in German and French between simultaneous bilinguals and monolinguals, and investigated the implications of any acoustic differences between languages and speakers on voice comparisons involving bilingual speakers.
