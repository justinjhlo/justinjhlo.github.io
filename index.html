Justin J. H. Lo

I am a fourth year PhD Candidate in Linguistics at the University of York and Part Time Lecturer at the University of Huddersfield. My research lies mainly in forensic phonetics, bilingualism and the intersection of these two areas, but I am also interested more generally in sociolinguistics and phonetics.

Outside work, I spend most time solving crosswords, living in fictional worlds and struggling to follow baking recipes.
